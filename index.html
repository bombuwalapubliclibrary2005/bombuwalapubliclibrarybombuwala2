About Our Library

Our Vision

Our vision is to make this public library the most exemplary and the greatest institution in the area in order to achieve sustainable development

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a productive library withing the modernized world prioritizing sri lankan identify with an intelligent and virtuous and reading community

Our History

This library was established in 1987 and officially opened as a public library in 1992. It initially operated in a small building and was later developed with a new facility. It received official registration in 1998, and additional improvements were made in 2006. Further developments took place in 2013, and modern services were introduced after 2019. Today, it serves the community with hundred of books and many active members.
